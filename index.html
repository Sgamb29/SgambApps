Sgamb Apps: Portfolio of Websites

Sgamb: "Just a developer from Canada. Started to learn coding in python with the Mimo App and then Harvards free online computer science course CS50 on Edx.org. Mostly develop sites and apps that I intend to use and find useful in my own life."

  1. Stretch Hold Timers

    This site is just a collection of timers. The countdown of seconds doesn't show on the site so you feel the time rather than watch the seconds. Drums or Chimes sound when the timer is up and the screen changes color. Note: Currently the audio on the website doesn't work on apple smartphones. There's also a sequence page with automatic timing and pictures showing the stretches for a simple routine.

    https://sgamb29.github.io/StretchHoldTimers/

  2. Music / Guitar Learning Game

    This site is mainly for helping to memorize where the notes are on the guitar fretboard, but there is also music theory information. Contains quizes and flashcards on fret positions and the sharps or flats in major scales.

    https://sgamb29.github.io/MusicLearningGame/

  3. Endless Typing

    This site is for typing practice and just the enjoyment of typing. Just generates random letters, numbers and optionally special characters to type. Tracks how long it takes you to type 1000 characters and how accurate you are, and then generates another 1000 characters when you reach the end.

    https://sgamb29.github.io/EndlessTyping/

  4. Simple Games

    This site currently only has one game that was created as part of a GameDevTv Udemy course. Looking forward to adding more games and updating this site as soon as I come up with more game ideas! (Use space bar for rocket thrust and a & d keys for tilt.)

    https://sgamb29.github.io/SimpleGames/

  5. Randomize My Day

    This site is for randomizing the order you go about doing your activities or tasks for the day. Adds some excitement and helps to make the decision on what to start with and what to do next!

    https://sgamb29.github.io/RandomizeMyDay/

  6. MP3 Audio Recorder

    This site is a simple audio recorder for making mp3 recordings.

    https://sgamb29.github.io/AudioRecorderMp3/

  7. Book Stats

    This site is for calculating your reading speed and time left for finishing books. Also it's for calculating exaclty how many pages to read in a day to finish a book in a certain time goal.

    https://sgamb29.github.io/BookStats/

  8. Mental Tools

    This site is a collection of tools and techniques that can help mental health and problem solving skills. Also contains some breathing exercises that can be useful for self regulation.

    https://sgamb29.github.io/MentalTools/

  9. Stop Chain Vape

    After switching to vaping from smoking this site was useful for not chain vaping or taking too many inhales in a short space of time (to avoid burnt vape taste). Basically just a simple timer that you can adjust and even make it loop. Again, the audio doesn't work currently on apple smartphones.

    https://sgamb29.github.io/StopChainVape/
